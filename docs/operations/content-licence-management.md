# Content Licence Management

## Licence lifecycle

1. Draft — editor creates licence terms
2. Pending review — submitted for verification
3. Active — approved and usable
4. Expired — validity period passed
5. Revoked — withdrawn by licensor
6. Superseded — replaced by newer version

## Re-verification triggers

- Licence approaches expiry (configurable warning period)
- Licence expires
- Licence revoked
- Source disputed
- Content version changed
- Policy version changed

## Evidence retention

Evidence records are retained for at least 7 years (configurable). Historical evidence cannot be deleted when referenced by a publication decision.
